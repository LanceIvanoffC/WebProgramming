About Lab 2

and absolute paths. Relative paths only show to way to get to the other from the file you are
currently in. Absolute paths show a way to get to another file from anywhere in the whole system.
The second thing is tables. How to construct tables and put content inside of them
whether its just or or hyperlinks.
